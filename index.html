Professional Overview

I started at Underwater Audio to lead iOS development for companion apps for their flagship Delphin swimming device. I worked in tandem with the Android team to build out the remote control and lap tracking apps. Three years later I took control of the Android team as well as iOS, including becoming the lead Delphin developer. As a part of our Delphin 2 release, I overhauled core Delphin software like the home screen and app installation.

In 2023, I was asked to assume the title of Chief Technology Officer. As CTO, I continued my work on the Delphin, improving user retention by focusing on onboarding and ease-of-use. I also worked behind the scenes by helping the marketing department automate our Amazon ad bids and verifying the tech details of packaging inserts and social media.

Project Overview

Most of my work was for the Delphin, Underwater Audio's flagship product. It runs Android 10 on a 1.5" touchscreen.
