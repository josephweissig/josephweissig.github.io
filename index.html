Delphin

The Delphin is the flagship swimming companion from Underwater Audio. The 1.5 inch touchscreen and Android 10 OS allows for installation of apps like Spotify and Audible, providing users access to their music, podcasts, and audiobooks even while underwater.

Since 2018, I've been writing software for the Delphin, and in 2021, I oversaw all Delphin software development. The Launcher and Keyboard are both custom-made to accomodate the small screen, and the Lap Tracking takes advantage of the gyroscope and accelerometer to accurately count laps and provide voiced feedback in real time.

Delphin with screen off

Launcher

The Launcher is the Delphin's home screen, providing access to all installed apps and settings. It was designed to be simple and intuitive, allowing users to quickly find what they need without overwhelming them with options.
